Community Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts — Keeping our waste management system effective

DO's — Best Practices
  • Take clear, well-lit photos of waste before reporting
  • Provide accurate location details (address, purok, landmarks)
  • Select the correct waste category for your report
  • Include detailed descriptions of the waste type and volume
  • Update your profile with correct contact information
  • Follow your barangay's collection schedule
  • Respond to admin notes or verification requests
  • Separate waste properly before disposal
  • Report only actual, existing waste issues in your area
  • Engage respectfully in community discussions
DON'Ts — Strictly Prohibited
  • Don't upload fake, stock, or AI-generated images
  • Don't submit duplicate reports for the same issue
  • Don't report non-existent or false waste problems
  • Don't use offensive language in descriptions or comments
  • Don't spam the system with multiple identical reports
  • Don't claim points for someone else's report
  • Don't share personal information of other users
  • Don't upload inappropriate or unrelated images
  • Don't harass or bully other community members
  • Don't misuse the reporting system for personal gain
Waste Sorting Guidelines
  • Biodegradable: Food scraps, garden waste, leaves, compostable items
  • Recyclable: Plastics, paper, glass bottles, metal cans, cardboard
  • Residual: Mixed waste, soiled materials, diapers, sanitary products
  • Special Waste: Furniture, construction debris, bulky items
  • Hazardous: Chemicals, paint, batteries, medical waste, electronics
  • E-Waste: Phones, computers, broken appliances, cables
Consequences of Violations
  • First offense: Warning notification + temporary posting restrictions
  • Second offense: Points deduction (50-100 points)
  • Third offense: Account suspension (7-30 days)
  • Severe violations: Permanent account ban
  • Flagged reports are reviewed by admins
  • False reports may result in point reversals
